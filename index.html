Indigenous Ecology

Guardians of the Forest

The Jarawa people of the Andaman Islands have inhabited their forests for over 55,000 years. Their deep ecological knowledge sustains one of Earth's last pristine ecosystems. Their sovereignty and isolation are protected by Indian law.
